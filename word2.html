Clever


Definition: Quick to understand, learn, and devise or apply ideas; intelligent.

Usage in Sentences:

  • She gave a clever answer to the tricky question.
  • The magician's trick was quite clever.
  • He's very clever with technology.
  • It was a clever way to solve the problem.
  • Her clever plan saved the day.
Synonyms Antonyms

Intelligent Dull
